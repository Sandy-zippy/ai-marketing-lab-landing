# AI Marketing Labs: Scorecard Funnel Ads Plan

**Offer:** Founding Cohort (6 weeks, 6 agents) + 1-on-1 done-for-you. **Front door:** the scorecard quiz "Which AI marketing agent should you build first?" **Budget:** lean launch, ₹20-30k.

**Research grounding (what is working now):** quiz funnels convert 30-50% of starters to leads vs 3-10% for static lead magnets; 4-7 questions is the sweet spot (ours is 7). Winning ad pattern: sell the RESULT BUCKET ("find out which agent"), not the offer behind it. Priestley 2026: educate and build confidence, do not pressure; scarcity only works when real (ours is: 100 seats, 37 taken). AI-agent education offers win on hands-on outcomes ("you build real agents"), not certificates; our edge vs all of them is "you build on YOUR OWN marketing work and keep the agents." Post-Andromeda Meta: creative IS the targeting; broad + diverse concepts beat interest micro-slicing; 50 events/week per ad set is the learning constraint.

## 2. Platform plan

**Primary channel: Meta (feed + reels). Put 100% of the ₹20-30k on Meta for month one.** The quiz is an impulse-friendly, low-friction click, made for interruption media. Google Search at this budget buys 150-300 clicks swamped by tool ads; LinkedIn CPCs in India run 3-5x Meta. Do LinkedIn and X organic (build-in-public posts to the quiz), not paid, until the funnel proves CPL and quiz-to-waitlist rate on Meta.

**Angle-to-placement:**
- Meta feed (statics): A2 quiz, A4 anti-course, A6 scarcity, A9 DFY fork, A11 syllabus.
- Meta reels/video: A1 scattered ChatGPT, A3 proof, A5 follow-up leak, A7 status, A8 stack flex, A12 WhatsApp.
- Google Search (later, ₹5k/mo max, only after Meta CPL proven): brand terms + "AI marketing agents course India", "n8n marketing automation course". Bottom-funnel catcher.
- LinkedIn (organic only for now): A3, A7, A9 as founder posts.

**Budget split (₹25k reference, 30 days, ~₹830/day):**

| Line | Daily | Monthly | What |
|---|---|---|---|
| Cold prospecting, 1 ad set, Leads objective | ₹650 | ₹19,500 | One consolidated ad set, 6-8 active ads (test-in-main). Do NOT split into multiple cold ad sets; nothing exits learning at this spend |
| Retargeting | ₹180 | ₹5,500 | One ad set, the 3-track sequence in section 5 |

**Learning-phase math:** 50 events/week per ad set. At ₹650/day cold = ₹4,550/week, which exits learning only if CPL stays at or under ~₹90. Optimize the pixel Lead event on quiz COMPLETION (thank-you page); if CPL runs above ~₹150 in week one, drop the optimization event to quiz START to feed volume. Never optimize for LPV to feel busy.

**Geo:** Launch India-only (the budget cannot feed two auctions). Broad-India, Advantage+ audience expansion ON, national lead-gen with a strong creative signal. Add a US/UK/CA/Gulf ad set only when budget grows past ₹60k/mo.

**Audiences:** Cold = broad 24-45 + a light interest seed (Digital marketing, Facebook Ads Manager admins, Small business owners, Entrepreneurship, ChatGPT/AI interest); the quiz hook self-selects. Retargeting custom audiences to build day one: quiz page visitors 30d, quiz completers (upload the sheet weekly or CAPI the Lead event), IG/FB engagers 90d, waitlist joiners. Lookalike: 1% India on quiz completers once the list crosses ~1,000. Exclude quiz completers from cold, always.

**Tracking prerequisites before spending:** Pixel Lead event on the thank-you page (not the form page), CAPI relay of hashed email+phone from the sheet (n8n job), UTMs on every ad link, dedupe event_id. Wire quiz-start and quiz-complete as PostHog events so abandoner retargeting has a real definition.

## 4. Creative concepts

Brand note: AI Marketing Labs uses its OWN palette, not ZippyScale lime. Teal #00A19B accent on dark green-black #0A0E0D, off-white #F5F5F0 text, warm tint #FFE5A3. Reels follow AIML grammar: Pixelify Sans for big hero hooks only (wide spacing, avoid C/O/D words), Figtree for captions, no vendor logos that render broken, real stack proof only.

**C1. "The Scorecard Phone" (static 1080x1350 + 1080x1080).** Dark canvas, centered phone showing quiz Q1 with the teal progress bar; four teal-outlined chips floating: LEAD, FOLLOW-UP, CONTENT, CAMPAIGN. Hook top: "Which AI agent should you build first?" Sub in warm tint: "7 questions. 2 minutes. Free build plan." Bottom: wordmark + teal "Take the scorecard" pill. Pairs with Ad 1.

**C2. "Terminal Proof" (reel 20-30s, screen-capture led).** Full-frame screen recording of the real Hermes fleet: n8n canvas, then a terminal running an agent task, then the WhatsApp/email output it produced. Sandy talking-head open and close (selfie), screen full-frame in the middle, never b-roll over the face. Spoken hook (first 2s): "This is not a demo. This is the fleet that runs my agency." On-screen: Pixelify ALL-CAPS "REAL AGENTS" at open; Figtree captions, teal keyword pops. End card: "Which one should YOU build first? Scorecard in bio." Pairs with Ad 2 / A3.

**C3. "Course vs Cohort" split (static).** Vertical split. Left greyed-out: a stack of certificates, muted label "A COURSE: notes, a certificate, a folder you never open." Right dark with teal glow: 6 agent node-cards wired together, label "THE COHORT: 6 working agents. Yours." Hook: "Leave with agents, not notes." Pairs with Ad 3.

## 5. Retargeting sequence

One retargeting ad set (₹150-200/day), three tracks. Refresh creative when frequency passes 3.5.

**Track 1: Quiz abandoner** (visited quiz, no Lead event, 14d). Kill friction, restate the small ask, no cohort selling. Ad: C1 variant. Text: "You opened the scorecard and life happened. It is 7 questions, saved where you left off. Two minutes and you get your build plan: which agent first, and why." Headline: "Your build plan is 7 questions away." CTA: Take Quiz.

**Track 2: Quiz completer, no waitlist action** (Lead fired, not on waitlist, 3-21d). Two-step. Step 2a (days 3-10): C5 roster. Text: "You know which agent you should build first. The Founding Cohort is where you actually build it: live, one build a week, on your own business, on the stack I run my agency with. You keep everything." Headline: "You got your result. Now build it." Step 2b (days 10-21): C6 counter, Ad 5 copy. Headline: "Founding cohort happens once."

**Track 3: 1-on-1 warm** (waitlist joiners + completers whose answers signal budget/no-time, e.g. agency owner/founder segment, uploaded as a custom audience). Sell the outcome, not the build. Minimal dark static, one line: "Do not want to build it yourself?" Text: "Some founders want the agent system without the six weeks. That is the 1-on-1 track: we build your whole marketing agent fleet for you, end to end, then hand you the keys. Fewer spots than the cohort, by design. Reply to your waitlist email or ping us on WhatsApp." Headline: "We build it. You run it." CTA: Send WhatsApp Message.

**Kill/scale rhythm:** review at 48h cadence, kill any ad at CPL 30%+ above target after ₹2k spend, scale winner +20% every 3-4 days, refresh retargeting creative every 14 days. Launch everything PAUSED, activate after preview review.

**Key decisions flagged:** Meta-only and India-only for month one; ONE consolidated cold ad set with 6-8 ads (not angle-split ad sets); optimize on quiz completion with a fallback to quiz-start if CPL runs hot; creatives use the AIML teal/dark palette, not ZippyScale lime; all seat numbers in ads must track reality (37/100 today) and update as seats fill.
